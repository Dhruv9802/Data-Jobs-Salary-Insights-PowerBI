## Job Data Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

This folder contains the Power BI dashboard created to analyze job market data, focusing on job roles, salaries, and degree requirements.

### Dashboard Insights
- Highest paying job roles in the data domain
- Job roles with and without degree requirements
- Median yearly and hourly salary analysis
- Job count distribution by role
- Country-wise comparison of salaries
- Monthly trends in job postings and salaries
- Detailed job-level salary and company information

### Key KPIs
- Total Job Count
- Median Yearly Salary
- Median Hourly Salary
- Salary Star Rating

### Tools & Techniques Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX Measures
- Interactive visuals and filters
